Questions

Frequently asked questions.

If you have a question not answered here, email us at hello@draftpaid.com

Do I really need to create an account?
+
No — never, on the free plan. Open DraftPaid, create your invoice, download your PDF. No email, no password, no sign-up. We built DraftPaid specifically because we were frustrated by tools that gate basic features behind accounts.
Are there watermarks on free invoices?
+
No watermarks — ever. Free invoices include a small "Created with DraftPaid" credit line in the footer. It's discreet, professional-looking, and nothing like the giant stamps other tools plaster across your work. Upgrade to Pro to remove it entirely and send 100% unbranded invoices.
Where is my invoice data stored?
+
By default, your invoice data lives in your browser on your own device. We don't send it to our servers unless you create a Pro account and choose to enable cloud sync. Your financial information is your business — literally.
Will my pricing change if DraftPaid raises prices later?
+
Never for existing users. We make a public promise: if you sign up at $9/month, you stay at $9/month — forever. We've seen too many tools pull bait-and-switch pricing increases on loyal users. We won't do that.
Can I accept online payments through DraftPaid?
+
Online payment acceptance via Stripe and PayPal is a Pro feature coming soon. Free users can download and send PDF invoices, which clients can pay through their own preferred method.
What currencies does DraftPaid support?
+
DraftPaid currently supports USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, and JPY. More currencies are being added regularly. If you need a specific currency, email us and we'll prioritize it.
