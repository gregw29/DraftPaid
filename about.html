About Privacy Policy Terms of Service

The Story

Before college, I spent years working as a mechanic. I still do side work for friends and family — fixing cars, doing jobs for people. Every time I finished a job I needed to send an invoice. Simple enough, right?

Wrong. Every free tool I tried either wanted me to create an account just to download one PDF, slapped a giant watermark across my work, or was so bloated with accounting features I didn't need that it took longer to figure out the software than to do the actual job.

So when I started studying Computer Science in college, I built the tool I always wanted. Something dead simple. Open it, fill it in, download it. No account. No watermarks on the invoice. Done in under a minute.

That's DraftPaid. It started as a college project and turned into something I genuinely use and believe in.

My Promises

I make four promises to every DraftPaid user — free or paid. These aren't buried in fine print. They're the foundation of everything we build.

⚡
No account required — ever
Free users never need to create an account. Open DraftPaid and start invoicing immediately.
🔒
Your data stays private
Invoice data lives in your browser by default. We don't store your financial information on my servers.
💰
Grandfathered pricing
If you sign up at $9/month, you stay at $9/month — forever. No surprise price hikes for existing users.
🤝
I never hold your money
DraftPaid generates your invoice. When clients pay online, funds go straight to your bank via Stripe — never through us. No holds, no delays, no surprises.
